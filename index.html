Our Story

Crafted With Passion,
Served With Love

Born from a deep love for exceptional coffee, Ember & Brew started as a small roastery in 2019. Our founders traveled across continents — from the highlands of Ethiopia to the volcanic slopes of Guatemala — to source the finest single-origin beans.

Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality. We roast in small batches, grind to perfection, and craft each beverage with the precision of an artisan and the warmth of a friend.
